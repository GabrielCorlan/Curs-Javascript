What is Personal Data and how do we get it?

Personal data is information by which a customer or visitor can be directly or indirectly identified. Sharp Academy obtains this data as follows:

  • Directly from the customer or visitor

Sharp Academy collects personal data directly from the client or visitor, as a rule, that information provided after signing the contract or during its performance:

  • Name, surname, personal numerical code

  • Postal and email address

  • Phone number and more

  • Details of transactions carried out (purchased services, prices, payment methods)

Sharp Academy may also request other personal data for specific purposes, for example, when the customer or visitor provides a copy of an ID when concluding a study contract, with their consent.

In addition, in order to be able to provide the best products and services, Sharp Academy may sometimes request information regarding the customer's or visitor's experience while using Sharp Academy products and services. This information can be comments or suggestions as well as recommendations.

  • Automatically

In certain situations, Sharp Academy may also collect customer or visitor data through automated means. For example, personal data may be collected through cookies or when the visitor interacts with Sharp Academy social media pages.

Sharp Academy may combine various customer or visitor personal data from different sources (a website, an event, etc.) only to serve the interests of the customer or visitor in a personalized and legitimate way.

When you visit a website for the first time, a cookie that identifies your browser is sent to your device.

By using cookies you help us to improve the functionality of the website. These files mainly make it possible to recognize the user's terminal and present content in a relevant way, adapted to the user's preferences.

Cookies provide users with a pleasant browsing experience and support Sharp Academy's efforts to provide services that are as user-friendly as possible. Cookies collect information about how our users use the website and provide us with useful information about how we can improve the browsing experience.

If you want to block cookies, some functionalities of the site will be stopped, and this may generate malfunctions or errors in using our site.

We also use cookies for marketing purposes. These are used to exchange certain information with third parties with whom we advertise, to know how you arrived at our site. We may also use cookies to identify which parts of the site you are interested in. We then use this information to show you advertisements and pages that we think may be of interest to you, to personalize the way we communicate with you, or to personalize the content of the communications we send you. You can opt out of them if you prefer. Personalization of content means that it will include information that reflects the interest you have shown in the content of our pages or offers and promotions that we think may be of interest to you, all in order to improve how we respond to your needs.

As a Google Ads customer, we use Google Conversion Tracking, an analysis service provided by Google Inc. If you reach our website via a Google ad, Google Ads will install a cookie on your computer. The information collected from conversion cookies is used to generate conversion rate statistics for advertisers who have opted for conversion tracking.
